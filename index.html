Welcome to Bliss Resort Hotel!

Nestled in the heart of the beaches , Mombasa County, Bliss Resort Hotel is a haven of tranquility and luxury, offering you an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Our hotel boasts several fully furnished rooms, each designed with your comfort in mind.
When it comes to pampering our guests, our room service is second to none. Our dedicated staff is always ready to assist you with any request, ensuring your stay is as comfortable as possible. Whether it's a late-night snack or a rejuvenating cup of coffee, we are here to cater to your needs.

Experience the best staycation alongside the coastal beaches at Bliss Resort Hotel – where luxury meets hospitality.
